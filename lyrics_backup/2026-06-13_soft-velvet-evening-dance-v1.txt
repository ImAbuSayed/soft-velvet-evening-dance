[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fire pulling me close to you
Shadows on the wall painting a perfect view
Lost inside the motion of a quiet flame
A soft velvet evening gently calls my name

[Verse 1]
The sun goes down and fades into the night
I see your beauty glowing in the candlelight
Just two steady hearts beating in the dark
A gentle kind of magic lighting up a spark

[Pre-Chorus]
A lingering touch and I am falling deep
Into a dream I always want to keep
The quiet is a song we already know
I hold your hand and I will never let you go

[Chorus]
Oh, the night is wrapped in a golden haze
I want to lose myself inside this endless maze
Just a steady motion in a tender trance
Caught up in the middle of a soft velvet dance

[Post-Chorus]
[Ad-lib] (Sweeping me away in the gentle night)
A soft velvet dance until the morning light

[Verse 2]

Safe from the weather and away from the storm
Your whisper is the wind blowing through my mind
Leaving every worry and the world behind
